Case No. CIV-2024-11872

MOTION FOR SUMMARY JUDGMENT

Plaintiff Northgate Logistics, Inc. ("Northgate") moves for summary judgment
against Defendant Brightway Manufacturing, LLC ("Brightway") on its breach of
contract claim.

STATEMENT OF UNDISPUTED FACTS

1. On January 5, 2023, the parties executed a Supply Agreement under which
   Brightway agreed to deliver 10,000 units of component SKU-440 to Northgate.

2. The agreed delivery deadline was February 28, 2023.

3. Brightway delivered only 6,000 units, and did so on March 15, 2023 — well
   after the contractual deadline.

4. The Supply Agreement, at Section 7.2, states: "Time is of the essence, and
   any delivery after the deadline constitutes a material breach for which the
   buyer may recover all consequential damages without limitation."

5. As a direct result, Northgate incurred $250,000 in cover damages purchasing
   replacement components on the open market.

LEGAL ARGUMENT

Brightway's late and incomplete delivery is a material breach as a matter of
law. The contract's plain language forecloses any dispute. Northgate is entitled
to judgment and to its full consequential damages.
