SUPPLY AGREEMENT — EXCERPT
Between Northgate Logistics, Inc. and Brightway Manufacturing, LLC
Executed: January 5, 2023

Section 7.2 (Delivery and Remedies):

"Time is of the essence, and any delivery after the deadline constitutes a
material breach for which the buyer may recover consequential damages, provided
that such damages shall not exceed the total contract price and shall exclude
lost profits."

Section 9.1 (Modification):

"The delivery schedule may be amended in writing by mutual agreement of the
parties. Any such amendment supersedes the original deadline."
